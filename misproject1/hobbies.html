My Hobbies

Ice Skating

I fell in love with hockey when my grandpa and uncle were teaching me to skate. They both played hockey through high school and college, and my grandpa had coached my uncle's team. I look up to them as role models in my life and wanted to experience this sport for myself, so I started playing and never looked back.

Ice Skating is so important to me because I have met so many people through our shared love for this activity, and it has allowed me to bond with the role models in my life by learning from them and showing them my similar passions.

Hiking

One of my most memorable hikes was at the Elba Fire Tower in the summer of 2025. This hike includes a fire tower at the top of a hill, and in order to get to the top, you must climb over 700 stairs. I started training for this hike a month before I went on it because I knew it would be a challenge. I made the climb on August 9th at sunset. It was our most challenging hike and the most unforgettable one by far.

Favorite Hiking Locations

My favorite locations I've visited are:

  • The Elba Fire Tower
  • The Devils Punchbowl
  • Whitewater State Park

This activity means the world to me because it allows me to spend time with my favorite people doing an activity everyone loves and getting outside to explore the world and what it has to offer.
