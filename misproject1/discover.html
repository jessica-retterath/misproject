Why I Chose UMD

I chose UMD because I have always dreamt of living in Duluth. I am originally from Rochester, MN, which is very flat and visually boring. Whereas Duluth is full of nature, hilly, with many hikes and places to explore. After graduation, I am hoping to be able to stay here and create my life in this beautiful city, so I chose UMD to be able to establish myself here in hopes that it makes staying much easier in the future.
